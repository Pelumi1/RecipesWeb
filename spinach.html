Spinach and Feta Stuffed Portobello Mushrooms

Ingredients:

  • 4 portobello mushroom caps
  • 1 package frozen spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Remove the stems from the mushrooms and scrape out the gills.
  3. In a bowl, mix together the spinach, feta cheese, sun-dried tomatoes, onion, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stuff the mushroom caps with the mixture.
  5. Place the mushrooms in a baking dish and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
