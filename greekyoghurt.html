Greek yoghurt

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of greek yoghurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries(such as blueberries, raspberries and sliced stawberries)

Steps:

  1. In a small bowl, stir together the Greek yogurt and honey until well combined.
  2. Rinse and pat dry the mixed berries.
  3. Top the Greek yogurt with the mixed berries.
  4. Enjoy!
