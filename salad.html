Tuna Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of tuna, drained
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup of chopped celery
  • 1/4 cup of chopped red onion
  • 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the tuna, mayonnaise, red onion, celery, parsley, Dijon mustard, and lemon juice.
  2. Mix the ingredients together until they are well combined.
  3. Taste the tuna salad and add salt and black pepper to adjust the seasoning to your liking.
  4. Serve the tuna salad chilled or at room temperature.
