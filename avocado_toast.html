Avocado toast

Ingredients:

  • 1 avocado
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1/2 lemon
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: cherry tomatoes, red pepper flakes, feta cheese, etc.

Steps:

  1. Toast the bread slices to your desired level of crispiness.
  2. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop out the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Add the juice of half a lemon, olive oil, salt, and pepper to the bowl and mash everything together with a fork until the mixture is creamy.
  4. Spread the avocado mixture evenly onto the toast slices.
  5. Add your desired toppings, if any.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
