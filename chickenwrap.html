Chicken wrap

Ingredients:

  • Cooked chicken breast
  • Caesar dressing
  • Romaine lettuce
  • Grated parmesan cheese
  • Whole weat wrap

Steps:

  1. Chop cooked chicken breast into small pieces.
  2. Place the chicken in a bowl and add Caesar dressing to coat.
  3. Place romaine lettuce leaves on the whole wheat wrap.
  4. Add the chicken and Caesar dressing mixture on top of the lettuce.
  5. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the chicken mixture.
  6. Wrap the contents tightly and cut in half to serve.
  7. Enjoy!
