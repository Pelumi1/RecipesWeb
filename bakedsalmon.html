Baked salmon

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh dill
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Combine melted butter, lemon juice, honey, dill, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  3. Place salmon fillets in a baking dish and pour the mixture over the salmon.
  4. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until salmon is cooked through.
  5. Enjoy!
