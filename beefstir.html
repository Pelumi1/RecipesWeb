Beef stir fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb flank steak, sliced
  • 2 cups of your choice of vegetables (e.g. bell peppers, onions, broccoli, snow peas)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp grated ginger

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over high heat.
  2. Add the beef and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the vegetables and continue to stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. Mix cornstarch, soy sauce, oyster sauce, brown sugar, garlic, and ginger in a bowl.
  5. Pour into the skillet and stir-fry until the sauce thickens.
