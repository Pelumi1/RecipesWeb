Quesadillas

Ingredients:

  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped cooked chicken
  • 1/4 cup chopped cooked beef
  • 1/4 cup chopped cooked shrimp
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 2 tbsp. vegetable oil

Steps:

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion and bell pepper and saut√© for 3 minutes.
  2. Add tomatoes, chicken, beef or shrimp, and salsa. Cook for 3 minutes and remove from heat.
  3. Spread 1/4 cup of cheese on each tortilla. Spoon 1/4 cup of the vegetable and meat mixture on one side of each tortilla. Fold tortilla in half to form a half moon shape.
  4. Heat the remaining tablespoon of oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Cook quesadillas for 2 minutes on each side or until golden brown and crispy. Serve hot.
  5. Enjoy!
